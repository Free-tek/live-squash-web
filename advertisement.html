The problems with product and service advertisement

All marketers can agree that not all advertisement campaigns generate an increase in sales, despite the fact that they were created to, some business do not even believe in produc and service advertsement because they see it as a throw of a die and too much risk for them to get involved in. These views have spurned from the various problems that affect product advertisement.
According to Adtrader, in the previous year, 200 billion dollars was wasted in product and service advertisement and the major cause remains the fact that 80% of the audience that view Ad are not interested in the product that is being advertised to them (Claud Hopkins, Scientific Advertising). Advertisement is just sales manship and so if you are a marketer for a beard shaving cream and you go out for a door to door prospecting and on the first door you knock you find a lady in her early twenties open the door and after a short conversation you find out that she lives alone, will you spend the next 2hrs convinving her to buy the beard shaving cream you sell? will she be buying them to shave off the hair on her dog? But this is what several advertisements do, sell to people who do not need their product and this has been established as the major problem in product and service advertisement, the higher the percentage a marketer can reduce his waste in advertising to the wrong category of audience, the higher his chance of a success on an advertisement campaign. But this is the inverse of what modern day advertisement offerss, i watched tons of sanitary pad adverts on TV in between my favorite cartoons as a young boy and they always came in seconds to when superman was about to destroy the vilan, comon.


Although i dont know what rank this problem occupies in product and service advertisement, i'll still classify it as a crazy one, and this is Lack of audience attention on advertisement campaigns. Marketer's cannot sell to an audience that is not listening. It has become a cliche for us to switch the TV or radio stations whenever commercials are rolled in or perhaps they are the perfect breaks for us to use our smartphones before we get back to our shows, please who parks their car on a highway to read what a dear marketer wrote on a billboard, who doesnt cancel ad pop ups when they surface on our phones, and when we visit a website our eyes are usually on the piece of information we seek to find and not the ads marketers have hanged around. So marketers get very little time to communicate with their target audience because all these form of ads practise interuptions ads and not permission ads.

Due to these factors product and service advertisement have year in and year out consistently returned low yield but our team for years now took out time to build a product that solved these basic problems.
