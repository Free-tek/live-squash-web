Free App's MVP ready for launch in UNILAG


Our team is happy to announce that our minimum viable product (MVP) is set for launch at the University of Lagos upon resumption for the next academic session. We have uniquely designed a product that tests our business ideas as also solves problems in this campus.

Some of the services the app is set to offer are:
1. Promo codes to buy various meals for a cheap price, as well as a free delievery to any location on campus or its environs. The menu includes Ofada Rice, Jollof Rice and Pounded Yam.


2.Promo codes for a free bus ride ticket from CITS to Yaba or Bariga from 4-6pm on Mondays to Fridays.


3.Promo code for a one week wash laundry plan for just N300 plus free pick up and delivery.


4.Promo codes to get cheap + Free movie tickets at Ozone cinema.


5.Promo codes to buy various fashion items and awesome price cuts.


6. To the feature I like the most, get paid for viewing Ads and the Free coins you earn can be used to buy meals, free bus ticket, pay for your laundry, movie ticket or airtime recharge and many more.

We believe in making the next second humans spend on earth better than the last and theses were the motives behind the development of Free App, we believe this app cannot only make life better for students on campus but is also very crucial in helping us develop our final product, one capable of scaling to solve global problems.
